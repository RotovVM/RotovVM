## 👨‍💻 About me

My path to data analytics grew naturally from medicine and public health research. I work at the **N.A. Semashko National Research Institute of Public Health**, where I analyze regional healthcare data, perform statistical analysis, prepare analytical reports and presentations, and participate in the development of software for public health monitoring.

My experience includes projects for the **Accounts Chamber of the Russian Federation**, automated comparison of Russian regions by medical and social indicators, and internal software solutions for health monitoring and reporting automation. I am especially interested in analytics at the intersection of **healthcare**, **public policy**, and **decision support**.
